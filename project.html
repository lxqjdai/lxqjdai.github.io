Intelligent Agent: Pac Man Search
Adapted from the Berkeley Pac-Man Assignments originally created by John DeNero and Dan Klein. This is an AI course project to implement an agent to undertake specifical tasks, as well as to find the path to the end in mazes. What we do are: 1) implement path-search algorithms for DFS, BFS, UCS as well as A*, and 2) design effective heuristics.




3D Deformation transfer
Aim to tansfer the deformation of two 3D face mesh to another face mesh. Proposed a method can deform a 3D mesh and transfer the deformation automatically. Obtained the 3D mesh of the source face image with VRN-net and Marching Cube algorithm. Deformed the source mesh with 3D Thin Plate Spline algorithm and target feature. Transferred the deformation to other 3D meshes by minimizing an energy function.
